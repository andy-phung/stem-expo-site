About
Ever wondered what our STEM clubs do every year?
At the STEM Expo, you’ll get to see what each of Indy’s STEM clubs have to offer through fun activities in an expo-style booth session.
Afterwards, a panel of IHS alumni in STEM majors will speak on their own experiences in these clubs and other activities they did in high school.
There’ll be food, games, raffle prizes, free swag, and plenty of opportunities to connect with new people, so RSVP now!
Hope to see you there :)
