About\LARGE \textbf{About}About
Ever wondered what our STEM clubs do every year?
At the STEM Expo, you’ll get to see what each of Indy’s STEM clubs have to offer through fun activities in an expo-style booth session, followed by a panel of IHS alumni who were a part of these clubs.
There’ll be food, games, raffle prizes, free swag, and plenty of opportunities to connect with new people, so RSVP now!
Hope to see you there :)

Frequently Asked Questions\LARGE \textbf{Frequently Asked Questions}Frequently Asked Questions
How do I register? Are there any fees?
Register here! No——admission is completely free, and includes swag, food, and entry into our raffle.
How long is the Expo?
Around 2.5 hours! The Expo will be held from 4:00 PM to 6:30 PM.
Where is C-Commons?
C-Commons is located near the theater and staff parking lot towards the south end of campus. You can refer to this map of IHS.
What am I supposed to do at the Expo?
At the STEM Expo, you’ll have the opportunity to learn about the STEM clubs and STEAM Academy classes we have at Independence.
You should watch club and academy demonstrations and presentations, participate in club-organized activities at their booths, and listen to our panel of alumni who have gone on to study STEM at top universities while gaining industry experience.
And of course, try to meet new people while you’re at it :)
Will there be food?
Yes! Food will be served from 6:00 to 6:20 pm. You must have filled out our RSVP form to get food.
How does the raffle work?
You’ll receive one raffle ticket upon RSVPing. You can get more if other attendees use your referral code; both you and them will receive one additional raffle ticket.
